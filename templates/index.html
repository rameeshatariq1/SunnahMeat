About Us

Rooted in Faith,
Driven by Purpose

Sunnah Meat was founded in 2016 with a simple mission: to serve humanity with dignity, compassion, and sincerity. We believe that no family should sleep hungry, and every act of giving should create real impact in the lives of those in need.

We are a Pakistan-based humanitarian organization operating in Lahore, Faisalabad, and Multan. Our dedicated volunteers, trusted partners, and support teams work together to provide food assistance, community support, and relief programs to underserved families throughout the year.

Beyond seasonal campaigns, we actively support initiatives focused on feeding the hungry, helping orphan families, distributing ration packages, supporting shelter communities, and improving access to clean water and education.
